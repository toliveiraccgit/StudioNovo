Thiago Oliveira

Thiago Oliveira had 15 years working as a design.

He worked for some of the Brazil’s main agencies of Ux and design consultancy. Throughout his career he already lead teams in projects for Coca-Cola, Samsung, Google, Absolut, Creditas, Ambev, Cyrela, among others.

The Studio

We are a studio specialized in experience design for digital products, which could be a mobile app, a web page, or a site.

Business Model

We had as a business front our montage squad 100% dedicated and allocated for the client, growing the team capacity, and minimizing business risks. Through this model we already create digital experiences for Cyrela, Ambev, Webmotors, XP investiments.

Besides the squad montages, we also work in projects which approach a closed scope, where are defined the practices we will executed according with the project’s moment and the schedule based in weeks/sprints to conclude and deliver
