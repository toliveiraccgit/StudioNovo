The Studio

We are a studio specialized in experience design for digital products, which could be a mobile app, a web page, or a site.

The studio is located in Bahia, with a remote action team, attending clients in Brazil and worldwide.

How it was born

In the year of 2018, Thiago Oliveira and Anna Ladeira – business partner and Project Head – establish a studio.

Both worked for big consultancy agencies of Design & UX and realize about how startups tried to connect work and partnership, but don’t find a space for proximity. Today 90% of the studio’s clients are startups during any phase of operation, which comes from products nearly created to products around A series. Beyond startups, we have as clients brands with innovational proposes for digital products, who counts in our studio to build design identities for their culture.
